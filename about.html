About Us

Get to know Youth Advocates.

Who We Are

We are a youth led organisation that aims to empower communities to eradicate poverty, promote gender equality and good health.

Where We Work

Started in Zimbabwe, but who needs borders anyway? Now casually strolling through Zambia, Botswana, Malawi, and Kenya like it's just a walk in the park.

What We Do

We work tirelessly to transform lives and communities by empowering adolescents, women and minority groups.

How We Do It

We pioneer innovative grassroots programs that shift the focus from immediate behavioural factors to the structural drivers.

Where We Work

Youth Advocates, operating exclusively in Africa, addresses the challenges faced by over 425 million people in this vast region. In the rural areas of Kenya, Malawi, Botswana, Zambia, and Zimbabwe.

Overview

  • Country of Origin: Zimbabwe
  • Other Countries: Botswana, Kenya, Malawi, Zambia
  • Districts Covered: Two hundred and seventy-one

A Pan African youth led organization powering health enterprising life styles. We empower adolescents and communities with knowledge, skills and resources for healthy, productive and fulfilling lives.

Let's Help Them Together

Together we can advocate for change.

Youth Advocates works with the government & communities to safeguard and promote the rights of children and young people. Over the years we have demonstrated experience with holistic programming for children, young people and women; integrating SRHR/HIV/GBV with livelihoods, climate action and social protection. We implement evidence-based programs that focus on impoverished areas in the community.
We pioneer innovative grassroots programs that shift the focus from immediate behavioural factors to the structural drivers that directly and indirectly increase young people’s exposures to both SRHR outcomes, HIV, poverty, gender inequality, sexual violence, public policies and lack of education.

Together we can :

  • Advocate for our youngones.
  • Say no to poverty.
  • Promote gender equality.
  • Promote good health & well being in communities.
  • Advocate for zero hunger.

Youth Helpline

Youth Helpline contact centre delivers free counselling, referral, information and expert-approved messages to over 900 000 mobile phone users across 27 districts in 3 countries on topics, including but not limited to sexual and reproductive health, social protection, education, and PSEA.

Women Empowerment

Youth Advocates Leadership Program

This online course aims to empower youths and adolescents with knowledge, communication, and decision-making skills to make informed choices regarding their sexual and reproductive health.

Empowering youth through confidential support and connection to services. Connect with us 24/7 via toll-free number 393. The Youth Helpline offers support and guidance round the clock.

Violence Against Women and Children

Standing against violence against women and children, we champion empowerment, support survivors, and advocate for a world free of gender-based abuse.

What We Do

We're here to support them on every step of the way.

Vocational Skills and Economic Strengthening

Visit Youth Advocates Adolescents Clinic for confidential counseling, sexual health education, and support services tailored specifically for adolescents' needs.

Women Empowerment

Empowering women at the core, our organization is dedicated to fostering leadership, providing resources, and advocating for women's rights.

Youth Advocates Leadership Program

This online course aims to empower youths and adolescents with knowledge, communication, and decision-making skills to make informed choices regarding their sexual and reproductive health.

393 - Youth Helpline

Empowering youth through confidential support and connection to services. Connect with us 24/7 via toll-free number 393. The Youth Helpline offers support and guidance round the clock.

Adolescents SRHR

Empowering adolescents through comprehensive sexual and reproductive health services, ensuring informed choices and promoting well-being for a healthier future.

Violence Against Women and Children

Standing against violence against women and children, we champion empowerment, support survivors, and advocate for a world free of gender-based abuse.

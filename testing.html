Our Team Values

Principles that guide our work and team culture

Youth-Centered

We prioritize the needs, voices, and leadership of young people in all our work and decision-making processes.

Collaborative

We believe in the power of partnership and work closely with communities, government, and other stakeholders.

Accountable

We are transparent in our work and accountable to the communities we serve and our partners.

Innovative

We embrace creative solutions and adapt our approaches to meet evolving challenges and opportunities.
